Alloro Feature Friyay

Alloro now speaks beyond healthcare

Generic business accounts can use revenue-data wording while healthcare accounts keep the familiar PMS and referral language.

Hi there,

We expanded Alloro's wording layer so the same platform can support generic local-service businesses without showing healthcare terms.

For generic accounts, the dashboard now uses Business Hub and Revenue Hub language, asks users to upload revenue data, and talks about customers, leads, records, source channels, and revenue. Healthcare accounts continue to show Practice Hub, Referrals Hub, PMS data, patients, referrals, and production.

We also updated the in-app AI prompt layer so new summaries, actions, review replies, and Google posts follow the account type before they are generated.

Thanks,
