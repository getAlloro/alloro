What’s new in Alloro this week

Clearer insights, easier team management, consistent communications, and native DentalEMR import support.

Hi there,

Here’s a quick look at the latest improvements now available in Alloro.

See the work Alloro completed

Journey Insights can now show completed Alloro SEO updates beside the Google Visibility metrics they are intended to support. You can see what changed, when the work was completed, and the measurement window without treating the update as a guaranteed cause of future results.

Easier team management

Team Members and Pending Invitations are now easier to scan and manage. The updated tables keep names, roles, statuses, and actions organized across desktop and smaller screens.

Emails that look and feel like Alloro

Invitations, verification codes, password messages, billing updates, and system notifications now use one consistent, mobile-friendly Alloro design. Important messages are easier to recognize and read.

Native DentalEMR parser for DentalEMR clients

DentalEMR clients can now use a native parser built for DentalEMR exports and completed-treatment production rules. This provides a more direct and consistent path for calculating production and referral totals than applying a generic import formula.

We’re using the DentalEMR integration as the foundation for bringing native parser support to additional practice-management systems over time.

Thanks,
The Alloro Team
