1 · Do they find you?

Get found · impressions

Whether you come up at all when someone searches — on the map, in the blue links, and in AI answers.

Google listing category Building
The main label on your Google profile. Pick the wrong one and you don't show up for the right searches — it's the single strongest lever here.
Extra categories Building
The additional labels that widen what you show up for.
Name, address, phone match Building
Your details matching everywhere online. Mismatches quietly sink your ranking, and nobody tells you.
Citations / directory listings Building
Consistent listings across the many directories Google cross-checks to decide it can trust you.
Profile completeness Building
Every field on your Google profile filled in — services, hours, description, attributes.
Reviews as a ranking signal We watch
How many reviews you have and your rating. Google uses these to decide who shows up at all.
Open hours Building
You can vanish from the map entirely when you're marked closed.
Business-name keywords We watch
Keywords in your name help a lot — but stuffing them gets you suspended. Only a real legal name counts.
How close you are We watch
Distance to the searcher is the biggest single factor — you can't change it, but you can widen the circle you still appear in.
Words on your website Live
The content that tells Google what you actually do.
Can Google read your site Live
Whether search engines can crawl and index you at all — the gate under everything else.
Links to and within your site Building
Other sites linking to you, and your own pages linking to each other.
A page per service and town Building
A dedicated page for each thing you do and each place you serve.
Showing up in AI answers Building
Whether ChatGPT and Google's AI name you when someone asks. New, and growing fast.

3 · Do they choose you?

Get chosen · form submissions

Of everyone who opened you up, who actually raises their hand and reaches out.

Is the number even real Building
Making sure your visitor count isn't just bots and existing patients — so you fix a real problem, not a phantom. (Clean measurement is the piece we're building.)
Loads fast, on a phone Live
A slow page on a phone loses people before they read a word. We host it, so we keep it fast.
Trust in the first half-second Live
The gut feeling the moment your site loads — does this look safe and real.
The answers that decide it Live
Cost, insurance, what happens at the first visit, who they'll see — findable in seconds.
Real reviews on the page Live
The review text shown right on your site, not a link away where they'll wander off.
Easy to reach out Live
A simple form with no friction — and it runs through Alloro's own intake, not a form you rent elsewhere. (Counting exactly who raised their hand is the measurement piece we're building.)
Showing your availability Building
Making it clear you're open to take them. (The booking itself stays your front desk's job.)
How fast you reply Your team
Speed to a new lead makes or breaks it — that's your team. We make sure the lead lands clean and fast.

And then it loops — they come back and refer Building

After a good visit, a happy patient leaves a review — with your say-so first. That review becomes the thing the next stranger finds and trusts. Do it well and the funnel feeds itself.

  • We never promise a rank, a number, or an outcome. Everything here is designed to improve how eligible, trusted, and easy-to-reach you are — never a guarantee.
  • These are your numbers, not our scorecard. We show you the vitals and do the work. If a stage is healthy, we'll tell you to leave it alone.
