Why

The first backend foundation gives Alloro a market keyword base. This later plan turns it into an operating system: continuous enrichment, coverage monitoring, source health, and future data-source expansion.

What

Add operational jobs and internal visibility for Market Intelligence after the foundation has real data. This is not the first implementation pass. It exists to prevent the new keyword universe from becoming stale or unobservable.

Dependency: execute only after plans/06262026-market-intelligence-backend-foundation has run on pilot orgs and data quality is reviewed.

Context

  • The foundation spec introduces market_keywords and market_keyword_search_volume.
  • GSC harvest already stores daily query rows in website_builder.gsc_data.
  • Current app code does not store GBP search queries, competitor keywords, or DataForSEO keyword ideas.

Patterns To Follow

  • §21: background jobs must be idempotent, retried, service-backed, and logged.
  • §7.4: operational dashboards and jobs still read/write through models.
  • §20.2: tests cover failure paths and tenant isolation.

Reference file: src/workers/processors/dataHarvest.processor.ts for harvest operations and logging style.

Constraints

Must

  • Schedule continuous GSC enrichment after GSC harvest has data.
  • Track coverage internally: tracked keywords, unique GSC queries, matched queries, unmatched queries, matched impressions, unmatched impressions.
  • Track source health and data freshness for Market Intelligence.
  • Support future sources without changing the Market Intelligence Agent prompt.

Must Not

  • Do not expose keyword coverage to customers by default.
  • Do not import future external sources without separate provider validation.
  • Do not let continuous enrichment exceed the 300 approved keyword cap per location without a later approved spec.

Out Of Scope

  • Initial table creation and bootstrap. That belongs to the foundation spec.
  • Patient Journey UI switch.
  • GBP search queries until a concrete API/source is verified.

Risk

Level: 4. Continuous enrichment can grow cost and data volume across the fleet.

Risks Identified

  • Unbounded keyword growth. Mitigation: enforce caps, archive stale low-confidence rows, and require explicit approval to raise limits.
  • Operational noise. Mitigation: aggregate source health and alert only on actionable failures.
  • Future source ambiguity. Mitigation: each new provider gets its own spec and source adapter.

Blast radius: workers, monitoring, internal admin surfaces if added later, and Market Intelligence service behavior.

Pushback

  • Do not make this the first implementation. It depends on seeing whether the foundation produces sane data.
