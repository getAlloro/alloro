Why

The Websites overview Traffic and Leads cards hardcode the eyebrow as "Last 12 mo", but the charts plot the trimmed monthly series — leading no-data months dropped, capped at 12. A practice live for only a few months (e.g. Apr/May/Jun) shows 3 data points while the label still claims 12 months. The label misrepresents what the chart shows.

What

Both card eyebrows derive their month count from the actual plotted series length instead of a hardcoded 12. "Last 12 mo" becomes "Last 3 mo" when only 3 months are charted, and grows back to "Last 12 mo" once a year of data accrues. Each card reads its own series, so Traffic and Leads can legitimately differ.

Context

Relevant Files

  • frontend/src/components/website/overview/WebsiteOverview.tsx - renders the two OverviewCards with the hardcoded eyebrows
  • frontend/src/components/website/overview/websiteMetrics.ts - source of visitorSeries (trimmed, sliced to last 12) and leadSeriesCompact (trimmed leads); read-only, unchanged
  • frontend/src/components/website/overview/OverviewCard.tsx - eyebrow is a plain string prop; unchanged

Patterns To Follow

  • Inline pluralization/format helpers already live module-level in this file (fmt, durationLabel, gscDayLabel) — the new monthsRangeLabel sits beside them.
  • Sibling GSC card uses "Last 90 days"; the "Last N mo" voice is preserved for design consistency.

Reference file: frontend/src/components/website/overview/WebsiteOverview.tsx - existing fmt helper for the module-level format-helper shape.

Constraints

Must

  • Count = the series actually charted (m.visitorSeries.length / m.leadSeriesCompact.length).
  • Preserve the "Last N mo" abbreviated voice.
  • Cap at 12 and fall back gracefully when the series is empty.

Must Not

  • No new dependencies.
  • No backend / API changes.
  • No changes to computeWebsiteMetrics or chart rendering.
  • No refactor of unrelated card code.

Out Of Scope

  • The detail-modal chart captions ("Daily visitors · last 12 months", "Verified leads · last 12 months") — they describe the full daily/untrimmed window, a separate surface. Noted as a possible follow-up.
  • The "Search keywords · Last 90 days" GSC card.

Risk

Level: 1 (cosmetic label, presentational only)

Risks Identified

  • Series with interior month gaps count plotted points, not calendar span. Mitigation: acceptable — the label matches the number of points the sparkline actually draws, which is what the viewer sees; contiguous monthly data is the normal case.
  • Singular reads "Last 1 mo". Mitigation: the "mo" abbreviation is number-agnostic, consistent with "12 mo"; acceptable.

Blast radius: WebsiteOverview.tsx only — the new helper is file-local and the eyebrow prop is a string consumed solely by OverviewCard.

Pushback

  • "Last 3 mo" can read as a deliberate 3-month lookback rather than "only 3 months exist". Considered acceptable: it is honest about what is plotted and matches the card family's "Last …" voice. Alternative "Showing 3 months" was rejected to avoid breaking the established eyebrow voice.
