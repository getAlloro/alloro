## The three (from a 2026-07-07 brain-sweep)

### AutoSEO (getautoseo.com) — MEDIUM weight, closest direct threat
- **What:** AI content platform that auto-generates + publishes ~1 SEO article/day (1,500-2,500 words) from research on your business, customers, competitors; also builds backlinks via a network of other AutoSEO customers. Aims at both Google organic AND AI chatbots (ChatGPT, Perplexity).
- **ICP + price:** SMBs incl. **local services**, e-commerce, real estate, health startups. "Zero technical skills," integrates WordPress/Shopify/Webflow/Wix/Squarespace. ~**$400/mo**. Claims: "50-140% more organic traffic in 90 days," "94% median organic traffic increase in 5 months," "2,479+ businesses."
- **Why it matters (the threat):** overlaps Alloro's done-for-you-content-for-local-owners wedge at a MUCH lower price ($400 vs $2k flat). The cut: AutoSEO sells COMMODITIZED auto-published articles chasing a traffic number (the unprovable-lead-gen anti-pattern in `local-service-players.md`); Alloro's differentiation must be the specific, attributable, owner-seen result — not article volume. Watch it; don't compete on content-quantity.

### Levitate (saygoodbyetodiy.com → levitate.ai) — MEDIUM weight
- **What:** managed marketing platform (website, email, social, Meta/Google paid ads) executed FOR small businesses, with a dedicated success specialist + quarterly custom plans. "Your own marketing department for a fraction of the cost."
- **ICP:** small businesses 1-25 employees, referral/repeat-driven, no in-house marketing. Raleigh NC, 300+ employees, 8,179+ clients, strong reviews (4.9 Capterra).
- **Why it matters:** near-identical done-for-you-for-the-owner-who-can't-DIY positioning and the "not DIY, not a bad agency" framing Alloro also uses. A positioning competitor (same emotional wedge), broader-and-shallower channel mix vs Alloro's Attraction→Appointment focus. Landscape marker for how crowded the "handled marketing dept" pitch is.

### Brafton (brafton.com) — LOW-MEDIUM weight, landscape not direct
- **What:** full-service content marketing AGENCY since 2008 (blogs, video, design, SEO/PPC/social), enterprise + mid-market (Stanford, Preply, AppDirect). HubSpot Platinum partner. Now offering **generative engine optimization (GEO)** alongside traditional SEO.
- **Why it matters (light):** NOT Alloro's ICP (enterprise/mid-market, human-agency, custom pricing) — the classic labor-scales-linearly agency model. Kept only as a signal: incumbent content agencies are bolting GEO/AEO onto their menu, corroborating the AEO timing bet (see `aeo-visibility-tracking-signals.md`). Not a direct competitor for local owners.

## The read across all three
The done-for-you-marketing lane is crowded and cheaper (AutoSEO $400, Levitate SMB-managed, agencies everywhere). Alloro's edge cannot be "we also do your marketing" — per `local-service-players.md` the scalers won on ONE self-proving, attributable, owner-seen outcome + ICP discipline, not on breadth or content volume. These vendors are the commoditized field Alloro must NOT blend into.

## AI-native emerging entrants (2026, from the Notion Lead-Landscape migration 2026-07-08)
> Distinct from the legacy template vendors (PBHS/ProSites/DentalQore) and the DFY-marketing trio above. Closest-on-thesis threats. Weight: MED-HIGH. Source: Notion "Lead Landscape Analysis"; vendor claims are claims, not independently re-verified.
- **Digital Floss** (digitalfloss.com; Dr. Stephanie Broussard, dentist-founder), the SHARPEST threat. Positioning near-verbatim Alloro ("Exceptional dentists deserve to be FOUND... invisible because the marketing isn't working"). Stack: on-site video (fly out, shoot months of content in a day), AI-optimized sites, PMS-integrated AI booking + voice agents, geo ads, funnels. Claims 50k+ monthly video views/client, 97% retention; Russell Brunson/ClickFunnels ecosystem. Builds for GENERAL dentistry, so the endo/ortho SPECIALIST lane is the opening.
