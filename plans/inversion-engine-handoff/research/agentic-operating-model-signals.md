## "A field guide to Claude Fable: finding your unknowns" (Anthropic/claude.com), filed 2026-07-07

- **What:** working with a strong agentic-coding model, output quality is now bottlenecked by the HUMAN'S ability to surface unknowns, not the model's capability — "the first model where the quality of the work is bottlenecked by my ability to clarify its unknowns." - **Why keep (corroboration of our operating model):**
